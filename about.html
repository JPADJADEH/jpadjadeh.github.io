ℹ️ My Bio

Hello! My name is JP Adjadeh.

John-Paul Adjadeh is a Ph.D. candidate in Political Science at The University of Texas at Dallas, specializing in governance and political institutions. He holds a B.A. in Social Work with Geography and Resource Development and an MPhil in Public Administration from the University of Ghana, Legon.

With a decade of experience as Principal Registrar at Ghana’s Ministry of Chieftaincy and Religious Affairs, John-Paul has worked extensively on governance and conflict resolution. His research focuses on legal systems, democratic stability, and state repression, with recent papers under review and conference presentations. He is a Brian J.L. Berry Scholar, a recipient of the 2024-2025 Government and Political Science Fellowship, and a member of Phi Kappa Phi. He also serves as a reviewer for the Journal of Emerging Investigators.

His work bridges academia and policy, aiming to advance governance reforms and institutional development.
